Who We Are

Council of Legal Aid Group CDS Umuahia North is a group of Corps members who are mostly constituted of graduate lawyers and other young gradutes who take it upon themselves to ofer free legal services to members of the society in which they live as a means of giving back to the society.

What We Do

We give voice to the voiceless by standing in for them in court. We sensitize the society on their fundamental human rights. We induct new CDS members
